R&B MUSIC

What is R&B Music?

R&B music organinated in African American communities in the 1940s and consists of soulful vocals and smooth instrumentals. Some elements of this genre can be pop, soul, funk, and electronic. R&B stands for Rhythm and Blues.

What Instruments Does R&B Music consist of?

R&B music consists of electric guitar, bass guitar, piano, saxophones, synthesizers, and the keyboard

R&B Artists

  • The Weeknd - influenced by Michael Jackson's music and voice, The Weeknd has won numerous achievements. This can include having 28 songs hitting over a billion streams on Spotify
  • SZA - With hits like Saturn and All The Stars (With Kendrick Lamar), SZA has mostly explored R&b elements through her music. Her song "Kill Bill" was No.1 on the Billboard Hot 100 for 10 weeks.
  • Frank Ocean - With his strong and powerful lyrics, Frank Ocean is a fan favourite for Generation Z. In 2013, his album "Chanel Orange" won a Grammy for Urban Contemporary Album.

R&B Albums To Listen To

  • After Hours by The Weeknd
  • CTRL by SZA
